  - Before:
    ```scss
    $theme: mat-light-theme($primary, $accent, $warn);
    $dark-theme: mat-dark-theme($primary, $accent, $warn);
    ```

  - After:
    ```scss
    $theme: o-mat-light-theme($primary, $accent, $warn);
    $dark-theme: o-mat-dark-theme($primary, $accent, $warn);
    ```
